Use of functions in Matplotlib


plt.plot - Line graph
plt.bar - bar graph
plt.hist - histogram
plt.scatter - scatter plot
plt.pie - pie chart
plt.boxplot - box plot
plt.title - title
plt.xlabel - x-axis label
plt.ylabel - y-axis label
plt.legend - legend
plt.grid - grid
plt.savefig - save figure
plt.show - display figure
plt.xlim - x-axis limits
plt.ylim - y-axis limits
